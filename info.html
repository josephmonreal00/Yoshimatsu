Owners Story: Name...

People in our time are living with problems related to foods. I was born in the 1960's and grew up with the popularization of the fast foods, chemically enhanced foods and processed foods. Even in these times, I've always loved to cook and eat until my career as a fashion designer in Tokyo lef to hectic days and long, stressful nights. There was no time to cook for myself. During this time, I sustained myself mostly on junk and processed foods. I believe that lifestyle promoted cancer to grow in my body. After I was diagnosed, I began to research nutrition to heal and prevent illness. I'd like to make it affordable, because we eat everyday. Even though Yoshimatsu is a restaurant, we try to offer the foods like mom made at home; meals that are best for her family. That's the reason for Yoshimatsu. Yoshimatsu uses organic spring mix salad, tofu, carrots, natto and soybeans. Many dishes contain lots of veggies. Several Japanese traditional dishes may not contain many vegetables, but we'll be happy to accept your adding vegetable/tofu order. We use only canola oil, which is beneficial to the body. We do the best to order no MSG products fro distributors and we never add MSG. We are in a constant battle with the notion that we like to use organic and healthy food and also want to serve affordable. We will keep trying to use organic products, when we can. Have fun and take your time eating my healthy homemade dishes in my warm home of Yoshimatsu.
